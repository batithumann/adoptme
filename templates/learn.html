How Adopt Me works


If you are looking to adopt a pet

  • You can start looking at all the dogs and cats available for adoption right now.

  • If you wish to contact the people currently taking care of a pet you will need to create an account.
    If the people taking care of that pet is a registered shelter, their email will be visible.

  • After you contact them you will be able to see your messages using the link at the top of the page.


If you are looking to put a pet up for adoption

  • First you must create an account. You can create an account for your shelter or for yourself as a person. If you choose to create an account for a shelter then your email address will be public.

  • After you create your account, go to your account page, where you will be able to edit your details and find the link to see what pets you have put up for adoption and the link to put a new pet up for adoption.
